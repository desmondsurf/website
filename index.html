About Us

We are a surf company founded by Desmond, a passionate and adventurous young man residing in the vibrant coastal town of Katokro, Ghana. Our mission is to inspire and teach others how to surf while promoting a love for the ocean.

Desmond's deep connection with the ocean began during his childhood days in Cape Three Points. Growing up in a small fishing community surrounded by breathtaking beaches and powerful waves, he developed an immense respect and appreciation for the sea. Driven by his unwavering love for surfing and his desire to share its joys with others, Desmond decided to establish Katakro Surf Co. The company was born out of his belief that surfing has the power to bring people together, transcend cultural boundaries, and create unforgettable memories.

At Desmond and Katakro Surf Company, we offer a wide range of surfing experiences and services tailored to all skill levels, from complete beginners to seasoned wave riders. Whether you're looking to catch your very first wave or improve your technique, we will guide and support you every step of the way. We prioritize safety, ensuring that you have a fun and secure environment to learn and progress in your surfing journey. But our commitment extends beyond just teaching how to surf. We are deeply passionate about preserving the natural beauty of our oceans and coastlines. We actively engage in beach clean-ups, plastic reduction initiatives, and collaborate with local environmental organizations to create a sustainable future for our beloved surf spots.

Desmond and the entire Katakro Surf Co. team believe that surfing is not merely a sport; it's a lifestyle that fosters self-confidence, a connection with nature, and a sense of adventure. Through our surf lessons, surf camps, and community events, we aim to create a space where people can come together, learn, and grow while forging lifelong friendships. Whether you're a local or a visitor to Ghana, we invite you to join us on this incredible journey of surf exploration. Discover the thrill of riding waves, embrace the beauty of the ocean, and experience the joy of surfing with Katakro surf co.
